Years Of Experience

4CSS is distinguished by the experience and expertise of its professional team. 4CSS was established in 2015 to be a distributor of smart cards in Greater Cairo and industrial areas. Then it expanded its activities by providing a comprehensive security system for clubs, universities, companies, institutions and federations. One year later, 4CSS created a streamlined card development department and demonstrated rapid and successful growth. By focusing on market needs and quickly responding to those needs through customization solutions, 4CSS has completed its growth by exporting its systems to some Arab countries. 4CSS always maintains its relationship with its customers and their loyalty through technical support and strong customer feedback. 4CSS specializes in access control, attendance, security systems and smart solutions through smart cards.
